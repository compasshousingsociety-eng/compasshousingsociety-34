India's most trusted real estate partner — shaping communities with integrity, innovation, and heart since 2008.

To empower every individual and family to find their dream home with transparency, expert guidance, and unwavering support.

Our Journey

From a small dream to India's trusted real estate name

2008 – Foundation

Compass Housing was born with a single office and a big vision: to make home buying honest and simple.

2013 – Expansion

Opened 3 new branches across Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. Grew our team to 50+ real estate experts.

2018 – Digital Leap

Launched our AI-powered property matching platform, helping 10,000+ families find homes online.

2024 – Beyond Boundaries

Now serving 25+ cities with 500+ professionals. Committed to sustainable, community-first development.
