Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our home buying service

How long does the home buying process typically take?
The timeline varies based on your preferences and the market conditions. On average, it takes 3-6 months from initial consultation to closing. Our team works efficiently to expedite the process while ensuring you find the right home.
What are the costs involved in buying a home?
Costs include the down payment, closing costs, inspection fees, legal fees, and moving expenses. We provide a detailed breakdown during the consultation to help you plan your budget effectively.
Do you offer financing assistance?
Yes, we have partnerships with leading financial institutions and can help you secure the best mortgage rates. Our team will guide you through the loan application process and help you choose the right financing option.
Can you help with properties outside your listed cities?
Absolutely! While we primarily serve 25+ cities across India, we can assist with property purchases in other locations through our extensive network of real estate partners.
